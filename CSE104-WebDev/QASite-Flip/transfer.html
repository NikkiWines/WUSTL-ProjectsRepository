Difference in Schools

Wash U is known as one of the most challenging and competitve universities around.

The University is most well known for its medical school, though its engineering program, business program and achitecture program are also very highly reviewed.

The University sets itself apart through its incredible facilities, high quality professors, diverse specialities, and beautiful campus.

Why choose Wash U?

  • It's a phenomenal school
  • The culture around campus is very inviting and inclusive
  • The university is challenging, but will give you a great degree
  • The friends you make here will stay with you forever
  • The campus is absolutely stunning

Reputation

Though not a universally known university like many ivy leagues, Wash U has been an up and coming name for the last couple of years. The unviersity is becoming more well known due to its low acceptance rate, excellent campus, and phenomenal education quality.

Often called "The Harvard of the Midwest", Wash U is fastly becoming one of the most sought after universities.

As of 2015, Wash U ranked 15th on the US News and World Report Rankings, tied with Vanderbilt University. However, it is expected that Wash U's ranking will continue to improve.

Transfer Requirements

Wash U outlines all of it's transfer reuqirements online, and only a few to a handful of transfer spots are available per year.

As Wash U is a highly competitive school, it is expected that students who are trying to transfer in must meet a medley of university expectations. Transferring students' GPA must be equal to or higher than 3.5, and some divisions will require certain classes or GPAs in order to transfer into specific schools.
